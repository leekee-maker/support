Privacy Policy

NightSafari Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2026

1. Overview

NightSafari is a Safari extension app that helps users adjust webpage appearance for easier reading. We value your privacy and try to keep data handling minimal.

2. Information We Collect

NightSafari does not require an account and does not intentionally upload your browsing history, webpage content, or personal identity information to our servers.

  • Theme preferences and website rules are stored locally on your device.
  • If you purchase a subscription or one-time unlock through the App Store, Apple processes the payment information.

3. How We Use Information

We use the minimum information needed to:

  • Save your theme and rule preferences
  • Apply Safari extension settings
  • Restore purchase status when needed

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or intentionally share your local settings or browsing-related data with third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

NightSafari may rely on Apple system services such as App Store purchases and Safari extension APIs. Those services are governed by Apple’s own privacy policies and terms.

6. Storage and Security

Your settings are stored locally on your device and may be shared between the app and Safari extension through the system App Group mechanism. Please keep your device and Apple account secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

NightSafari is not intended for children and does not knowingly collect children’s personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

If the app changes in the future, we may update this policy and publish the latest version on this page.
